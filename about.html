About TerraShield

TerraShield Agriculture Insurance was founded to strengthen Malaysia’s farming community through modern, data‑driven crop protection. We focus on transparent risk assessment and affordable coverage to help farmers achieve long‑term stability and success.

Our Mission

To safeguard Malaysia’s agriculture by providing smart, fair, and sustainable insurance solutions that empower farmers and cooperatives to plan confidently.

Our Core Values

  • Transparency – Clear premium structure & coverage.
  • Innovation – Using climate and yield data for accuracy.
  • Partnership – Working side‑by‑side with local farmers.
  • Sustainability – Supporting resilient rural economies.

Our Product Highlights

  • 01. High‑Value Product

    Crop  Line  covers  rice  and  other  staple  crops .  High‑Value  Product  Line  focuses  on  rubber ,  palm  oil,  and  other  significant  crops.

  • 02. Income Protection

    Traditional  yield‑based  crop  insurance  covers  physical losses,  while ArgoShield  guarantees  a  minimum  income  level  by  covering  both  yield and  price volatility.

  • 03. Personalized Coverage Level

    Choose  coverage  tiers  :  50% ,  60% ,  70%,  80%,  or  90%  of  baseline  income. Real coverage level will be 20% higher than the chosen trigger level, with a maximum of 100% coverage
